The Hyper-Pareto Principle

Every account I've ever audited reveals the same pattern.

Roughly 5–10% of your ActiveCampaign activity drives 90% of your results. Sometimes it's two emails in a welcome series. Sometimes it's a single segment. Sometimes it's three automations doing all the work while twelve others produce noise.

The Hyper-Pareto audit finds those concentration points so you can double down on what works and stop carrying the dead weight.
